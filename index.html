Welcome to NAZAARABOX! We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about data collection practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we and our third-party service providers handle information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Data Collection Disclosure

While our App does not collect personally identifiable information directly, we use third-party services that may collect certain technical information for their functionality:

Third-Party Data Collection

The following third-party services integrated in our App may collect technical information:

This technical information is collected and processed by these third-party services according to their respective privacy policies.

Please note that these third-party services operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no personal data to secure. Any local data stored on your device remains under your control and is not transmitted to any external servers.
