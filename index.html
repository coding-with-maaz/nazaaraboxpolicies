Privacy Policy for NAZAARABOX

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Welcome to NAZAARABOX! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our privacy practices for the NAZAARABOX mobile application ("App"). Importantly, we do not collect any personal information from users of our App.

No Personal Data Collection

We want to clearly inform you that our App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you. Our App operates without requiring registration, personal accounts, or the collection of any personally identifiable information.

Please note that these third-party services operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Local Storage

Our App may store data locally on your device to provide essential functionality, such as:

All data stored locally remains on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no personal data to secure. Any local data stored on your device remains under your control and is not transmitted to any external servers.
